# 🗺️ What's Next for Cuhi Bot?

This is where we track our ideas and plans. We're a small team, so we focus on what makes the bot more reliable and useful for everyone.

---

## ✅ Phase 1: Making it Solid (Done)
We spent a lot of time "under the hood" to make sure the bot doesn't crash or lose data.
- **Pass 18 Audit**: Cleaned up the whole codebase, fixed indentation, and implemented static analysis checks for maximum stability.
- **Atomic Saving**: Fixed a bug where simultaneous edits could corrupt settings.
- **Better Downloads**: Switched to a real-time system that's much faster.

---

## 🏗️ Phase 2: User Experience (In Progress)
We want to make the bot easier to manage as you add more sources.
- **Account Rotation**: Support for multiple accounts so you don't get rate-limited.
- **Web Dashboard**: A simple browser view to see your history and manage links without typing commands.
- **Smarter Scheduling**: Pick how often each source gets checked.

---

## 🚀 Phase 3: Power Features (Planned)
The "cool stuff" for people who want more automation.
- **Plugin System**: Let you add your own Python scripts for custom processing.
- **Cloud Backup**: Send files directly to Google Drive or Dropbox instead of just Telegram.
- **Text Extraction**: Auto-read text from images so you can search your history better.

---

## 🌟 The Big Picture
Long term, we're looking at **AI-powered organization** (auto-sorting your media into themes) and **P2P backups** to make sure your archives are truly permanent.
